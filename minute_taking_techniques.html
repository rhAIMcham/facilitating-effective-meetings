Element Description Example
1. Context One or two sentences describing the topic discussed or the purpose of the agenda item. “Reviewed marketing budget options for Q1 launch.”
2. Key Points Summarise major points, facts or arguments raised by participants — not a word-for-word account. “Team compared vendor A’s and vendor B’s quotes; vendor B offers shorter lead times but higher cost.”
3. Decision Record any resolutions, agreements or conclusions made. Capture exact wording if possible. “Decision: Proceed with vendor A for pilot phase (approved unanimously).”
4. Actions Record tasks that arise, with responsible owners, due dates and dependencies. “Action: Raj to finalise vendor contract by 20 Oct; dependent on finance sign-off.”
5. Unresolved Issues Note open items, pending approvals or follow-ups for future meetings. “Pending: Final campaign visuals to be approved by brand manager next week.”
