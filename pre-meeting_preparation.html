Task Purpose / best practice
Access meeting link and tools early Join at least 10–15 minutes before start time. Test audio, microphone and video quality.
Open key materials Keep agenda, participant list and reference documents open on screen or printed for quick access.
Clarify recording policy Confirm whether the meeting will be recorded for transcription and if all participants have consented.
Set up note-taking tool Use OneNote, Google Docs, Notion or a shared Word doc that can autosave. If using transcription tools (Otter.ai, Teams transcript), ensure they’re turned on.
Familiarise yourself with attendees Note who is remote and who is in-room (important for hybrid settings).
Establish communication channels Coordinate on how online comments or raised hands will be tracked for inclusion in minutes.
