SMART element Description Example
Specific Clearly define what the meeting aims to achieve. “Agree on a timeline for product launch.”
Measurable Ensure progress or completion can be tracked. “Obtain final sign-off from all team leads.”
Achievable Make sure the objective can realistically be completed within the meeting. “Confirm three immediate actions for improvement.”
Relevant Align the objective with the meeting’s purpose and organisational goals. “Clarify key risks affecting delivery timelines.”
Time-bound Tie the objective to a specific timeframe, usually the duration of the meeting. “By the end of the meeting, finalise draft proposal.”
