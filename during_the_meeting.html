Aspect What to capture / how to manage
Introductions & attendance Record attendance by role — in-person vs. online. Note any technical issues that cause late joins or dropouts.
Agenda structure Follow agenda headings; record timestamps when new items start (useful for reviewing recordings).
Key discussion points Summarise main ideas, not every comment. Include both verbal and chat contributions where relevant.
Decisions made Document exact wording of any decision or resolution. Note if consensus, vote or facilitator’s decision.
Action items Record clear actions with owner, due date and dependencies. Verify ownership verbally to avoid ambiguity.
Chat box inputs Copy or summarise important points raised in chat — suggestions, links or agreements that influenced outcomes.
Polls / voting results Capture poll question, response options and outcomes (e.g., “80% approved proposed timeline”).
Screen shares / slides Note slide titles or document names discussed for easy reference later (“Refer to Slide 5: Budget Overview”).
Tech disruption Record any interruptions (e.g., “connection lost 10:25–10:30” or “audio distortion for 2 mins”). This provides context for any gaps.
Remote engagement Note efforts made to include online participants (“Facilitator invited feedback from online attendees”).
